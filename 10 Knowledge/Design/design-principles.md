# Design Principles

These principles govern the current design domain and the Press Rune.

## Vocabulary
- `Rune` is the named design identity.
- `Runestone` is the browsable UI that will render the Rune library.
- `Design Contract` is the project-specific instancing of a Rune.

## Axis set
The system uses eight axes:
- Density
- Temperature
- Luminosity
- Energy
- Geometry
- Chromatism
- Depth
- Posture

## Press interpretation
- Density: purposeful, not airy
- Temperature: controlled neutral
- Luminosity: light-first
- Energy: kinetic but mechanical
- Geometry: fully geometric
- Chromatism: near-monochrome with one structural colour
- Depth: restrained, border-driven
- Posture: assertive

## Motion grammar
- Motion should feel pressed, stamped, or landed.
- No spring physics.
- No organic ease.
- Structural motion beats decorative motion.

## Spatial grammar
- Border radius is zero.
- Borders are 2px.
- Depth comes from structure, not softness.

## Colour grammar
- Orange is structural.
- Neutral surfaces carry the system.
- No secondary accent is introduced unless the Rune says so.
